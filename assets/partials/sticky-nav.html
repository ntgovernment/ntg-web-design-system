Sticky navigation

Sticky navigation, or "sticky nav," is a UI design technique where the navigation bar remains fixed in place while users scroll down a webpage. This enhances user experience by keeping important links and menus constantly accessible, reducing the need for users to scroll back to the top. Sticky nav is particularly useful for long or content-rich sites, improving usability and encouraging prolonged engagement. It ensures that navigation options are always at the user's fingertips, making it easier to explore and interact with the website's offerings.
