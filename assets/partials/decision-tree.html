Decision tree

A decision tree web component is an interactive tool. It helps users map out and explore decisions.

It shows a series of choices or outcomes in a tree-like layout.

Each decision point (node) connects possible options (branches). Users can click on these branches to see how their choices lead to different results.

This component is useful for:

  • making decisions
  • creating flowcharts
  • explaining processes in websites and apps.
